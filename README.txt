Real-time radon detection helps you monitor air quality and protect your home from harmful radon exposure. 

This app seamlessly integrates the RadonEye RD-200 with your smart home system, providing timely updates and peace of mind so you can take action when it matters most. 

Enjoy a safer living environment with automatic monitoring and alerts, making radon safety effortless and reliable.

## Firmware Support

This app supports all RadonEye firmware versions:
- **Version 1**: First generation BLE firmware
- **Version 2**: Updated firmware with improved data structure
- **Version 3**: Latest firmware (extension of v2)

The app automatically detects the firmware version and uses the appropriate communication protocol.
